about us

At justiceLeague, our vision is a future where all Palestinians can access effective, sustainable, and locally-led healthcare systems, and fully realize their rights to health and dignity.

our mission

Through our programs in the West Bank, Gaza, East Jerusalem, and Lebanon, we collaborate with trusted and experienced local partners to bring this vision to life. Designed and delivered by Palestinians, our initiatives provide access to essential health services and build local knowledge and skills to address Palestinian health issues. In times of humanitarian crisis, we are prepared to respond rapidly with aid and assistance.

What We Do

Humanitarian Aid

We provide critical humanitarian aid to those in need, ensuring immediate relief and support during crises.

Healthcare Programs

We implement healthcare programs to ensure access to essential medical services and support local healthcare infrastructure.

Education Initiatives

We promote education through various initiatives, empowering individuals with knowledge and skills for a better future.

Community Development

We engage in community development projects, fostering sustainable development and improving quality of life.

our services

Community Outreach Programs

We engage with local communities to provide support and raise awareness on various social issues.

Donation Drives

Organizing and managing donation events to collect essential items and funds for those in need.

Emergency Response

During humanitarian emergencies, we provide immediate aid and support to affected communities.

Online Shop

Our online shop offers a variety of products, including handmade crafts, apparel, and accessories. Seventy percent of the proceeds from every purchase go directly to supporting communities in Gaza and the West Bank..
